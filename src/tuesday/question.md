3. Write a function that converts a temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit
    ### Formula:
        tFahrenheit = (tCelcius°C × 9/5) + 32
        
        Where 
            tFahrenheit: Temperature in Fahrenheit
            tCelcius: Temperature in Celcius
